About Me

Hi, I'm Randall. My friends call me Randy. In 2006, I attended my freshman year at Randolph-Macon College in Ashland, Virginia. In 2010, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Physics. In my free time, you'll find me strumming a guitar writing music, performing with my band, spending quality time with my partner and her dog, or my close friends.

Since then, I've managed restaurants, and developed my critical thinking, and technical skills working at Intuit and Apple, providing support for products such as QuickBooks Online, macOS, iOS, and Apple hardware.

A few years ago, I tasked myself to learn HTML and CSS in order to build a new version of my band's website. I loved the challenge I presented to myself. After working for Apple for a few years, I made the decision to pursue that challenge further, and become a web developer. Today, I develop using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, and MySQL. My website, and the examples you'll find on my website, are developed using all of these technologies.
